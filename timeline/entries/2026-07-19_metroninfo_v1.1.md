[MetronInfo](https://github.com/Metron-Project/metroninfo) v1.1 is released, building on the v1.0 schema from two years prior. The backwards-compatible update adds `AlternativeNumber`, an optional field for books with multiple numbering systems (such as legacy and relaunch numbers), and `CommunityRating`, which tracks average community ratings with an optional rating count.

Read the full announcement on the [Metron blog](https://metron-project.github.io/blog/metroninfo-v1-1).
